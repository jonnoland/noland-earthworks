Land Management & Forestry Mulching in Middle & West Tennessee

Noland Earthworks, LLC is a veteran-owned land management and forestry mulching company based in Vanleer, Tennessee. Owner Jon Noland operates a tracked forestry mulcher and personally handles every job across 35 counties in Middle and West Tennessee. No debris piles, no hauling, no burning — just clean, finished land.

Forestry Mulching

Forestry mulching grinds trees, brush, and stumps into a fine mulch layer in a single pass. The mulch stays on the ground as natural ground cover, preventing erosion and eliminating the need for burn piles or haul-away. Ideal for lot clearing, pasture reclamation, fence line clearing, and right-of-way work throughout Tennessee.

Land Management

Professional land management for residential lots, rural acreage, farm land, and development sites. Noland Earthworks clears overgrown brush, cedar thickets, and heavy vegetation using a tracked machine capable of handling steep slopes, wet ground, and dense timber that wheeled equipment cannot reach.

Vegetation Management

Ongoing vegetation management and brush control for properties, utility corridors, roadsides, and pipeline right-of-way. Regular clearing keeps fence lines open, pastures productive, and access roads clear year-round.

Site Preparation

Site preparation services clear vegetation and ready land for construction, development, or agricultural use. Noland Earthworks removes trees, brush, and stumps so your site is clean and ready for the next phase of work.
