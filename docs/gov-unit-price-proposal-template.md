## Section 3 — Inclusions

This proposal includes the following:

- All labor, equipment operation, fuel, and supervision required to complete the described scope.
- Forestry mulching of vegetation as specified, with mulch material left on site as ground cover.
- Mobilization and demobilization of equipment to and from the project site.
- Coordination with the owner's representative for pre-work site walk and final acceptance inspection.
- Compliance with applicable Tennessee Department of Environment and Conservation (TDEC) best management practices for land-disturbing activities, where applicable.

---

## Section 4 — Exclusions

The following items are **not** included in this proposal and are expressly excluded from the scope:

- Grading, grubbing, cut/fill earthwork, or any soil disturbance below the surface mulch layer.
- Debris hauling, off-site disposal, or burning of any material.
- Stump grinding below existing grade.
- Erosion control installation, silt fencing, or seeding of any kind.
- Utility locating (owner is responsible for calling 811 prior to commencement).
- Permitting, environmental surveys, wetland delineations, or archaeological clearances.
- Traffic control, lane closures, or flagging operations.
- Work on slopes exceeding [25%] without a written scope amendment and adjusted unit price.
