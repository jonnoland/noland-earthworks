## Purpose

This rate card establishes floor pricing for government and municipal contract bids. Every unit rate below already incorporates mobilization contingency, inspection overhead, payment-delay carrying cost, and a minimum 35% profit margin. Do not bid below these floors. Adjust upward for difficult terrain, remote access, or contract-specific compliance requirements.

## Mobilization

Mobilization is a separate line item on all government bids. Do not bury it in the unit rate.

| Distance from Vanleer, TN | Mobilization Charge |
|---|---|
| 0 – 50 miles | $350 |
| 51 – 100 miles | $600 |
| 101 – 150 miles | $900 |
| Over 150 miles | Quote individually |

For multi-day contracts, mobilization is charged once per mobilization event, not per day.

---

## Minimum Contract Value

$2,500 net of mobilization. Contracts below this threshold are not cost-effective given the administrative and compliance overhead of government work.

## Contingency Adjustments

Apply these multipliers on top of the floor rates when conditions warrant.

| Condition | Multiplier |
|---|---|
| Slope > 25% | 1.20× |
| Wet / saturated ground (seasonal) | 1.15× |
| Debris removal required (not standard mulch-in-place) | Add $350/acre |
| Prevailing wage requirement | Add 12% to labor component |
| Bonding required (performance/payment bond) | Add bond premium + 5% |
| Inspection hold points (work stops for inspector) | Add $150/day standby |
| Night or weekend work required | 1.30× |

## Payment Terms (Government Contracts)

Standard net-30 from invoice date. For contracts over $25,000, request a 25% mobilization payment prior to start. Include a retainage release clause: retain no more than 5% and release within 30 days of substantial completion.

If the contract specifies net-60 or longer, increase all unit rates by 8% to account for carrying cost.

---

## Exclusions (Always State in Bid)

The following are not included in any unit rate and must be addressed as separate line items or excluded from scope:

- Grading, grubbing, or earthwork of any kind
- Debris hauling or off-site disposal
- Stump grinding below grade
- Erosion control installation or seeding
- Utility locates (call 811 — owner responsibility)
- Permitting of any kind
- Traffic control or lane closures
- Environmental surveys or wetland delineations
